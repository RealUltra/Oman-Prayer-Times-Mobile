Quick Summary

Oman Prayer Times is designed to show prayer times for cities in Oman with minimal data use.

No account required
No ads or analytics
No Android location permission
City/date sent only to retrieve prayer times

Information We Use

The app does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or payment information.

The app lets you choose a city and date so it can show prayer times for Oman. That selected city/date information is sent to our API to retrieve prayer-time data.

The app does not request Android location permission and does not use GPS or background location services in the current version.

The app also stores settings locally on your device, such as your selected city, theme, and iqamah preferences, along with cached prayer-time data for offline access.

How We Use Information

We use selected city/date information only to provide the app's core features:

  • show prayer times
  • calculate the next adhan or iqamah countdown
  • save and load your app preferences
  • cache previously loaded prayer times for offline use

We do not use this app for ads, analytics, marketing, user profiling, or account management.

Sharing

The app sends the selected city/date to our API so it can fetch prayer-time data from the Ministry of Endowments and Religious Affairs (MERA) website.

We do not sell your personal information.

Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the app's features, support the service, resolve issues, and meet legal or security obligations.

Data stored on your device remains there until you clear the app data or uninstall the app.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encrypted network connections where supported by the service, to protect information in transit and on our systems.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at the app's published policy location.
